About the Product

Built for the Indian Cashew Industry

The IXE Tronics CULTI 7 Digital Moisture Meter is a handheld device that provides a direct readout of moisture content levels of produce. It is used to guide farmers, traders, and processors in determining whether the produce is safe for processing or storage.

Designed and manufactured in Mangalore, India — the CULTI 7 has been a trusted tool in the cashew industry since 2021, combining rugged build quality with precise measurement technology.

Standard Operating Procedure

How to Use CULTI 7

Simple steps to get accurate moisture readings every time.

01

Clean & Power On

Ensure the inner tube is clean and free from residue. Switch on the meter and check battery level.

02

Wait for Calibration

The device will self-calibrate, showing 'calib' on the display. Do not place any sample inside during this step.

03

Fill the Sample

When prompted with 'Fill Sample', weigh the recommended quantity (700g RCN / 600g NW) and fill to the brim. Shake gently.

04

Read & Repeat

Check the moisture reading on the OLED display. For best accuracy, repeat the procedure multiple times.
